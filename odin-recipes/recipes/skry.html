Skyr with Honey & Nuts

Skyr is a traditional Icelandic dairy product, similar to yogurt, that was enjoyed by Vikings. It is high in protein and can be enjoyed with a variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2c Skyr or Active Yogurt
  • 4 tbsp Honey
  • 1/2c Mixed Nuts, chopped
  • 1/2c Berries

Steps

  1. Divide the skyr among four serviing bowls.
  2. Drizzle each bowl with honey and top with chopped nuts and berries.
  3. Serve immediately.
